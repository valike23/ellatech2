ellatech ACT: Subsea Brownfield Upgrade

The client required an extension of their existing subsea control system situated in a subsea tieback field in the UKCS. Due to obsolescence and limit of operational spares, a threat to existing system maintenance and availability existed.

Using our retrofit expertise and capabilities, ellatech provided a fully redundant co-exist solution which secured not only the production capability but also provided the opportunity to plug in for future field extension opportunities.
